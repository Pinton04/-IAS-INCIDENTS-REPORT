🩹 First Aid Reminder

  • ✅ Stay calm and ensure your safety first.
  • 📞 Call emergency services if the injury is serious.
  • 🧼 Stop bleeding using clean cloth or bandage.
  • 🧊 Apply cold compress to reduce swelling if needed.
  • 📍 Record what happened and who was involved.
